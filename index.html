What is Mirror Work?

The most powerful healing tool
was always in front of you

Mirror Work is a simple, deeply transformative practice: you look at yourself — really look — and you speak with kindness. That's it. And yet for most women, it's the hardest thing they've ever done.

Developed by Louise Hay, author of You Can Heal Your Life, Mirror Work cuts through the noise of productivity-based wellness and goes straight to the root: how you talk to yourself when no one's watching.

Over 10 guided sessions, Dr. Archana and Dr. Seema will hold space for you to do what years of self-improvement strategies haven't: meet yourself with compassion.

Questions

Answered honestly

What exactly is Mirror Work?

Mirror Work is a practice developed by Louise Hay where you use your own reflection as a tool for self-compassion and inner healing. It sounds simple — look at yourself and speak kindly — but most people find it profoundly uncomfortable at first. That discomfort is exactly where the growth is.

Do I need experience with meditation or mindfulness?

No prior experience required. These sessions are designed to be accessible. The only thing you need is a willingness to show up. If you're new to mindfulness, this is actually an ideal entry point — it's practical, personal, and immediately applicable to daily life.

What happens in each session?

Each session is 60 minutes on Zoom. Dr. Archana and Dr. Seema guide you through a combination of reflection, guided practice, group sharing (optional), and a take-home practice for the following day. You'll never be forced to share anything you're not ready for.

Can I join if I'm not a FitNova client?

Yes, absolutely. This series is open to anyone. FitNova's mindfulness sessions have always been a standalone offering. You do not need to be on any FitNova health program to attend.

What if I miss a session?

We understand life happens. Recordings will be shared in the WhatsApp group for paid participants. However, the live experience — the group energy, the shared reflection — is what makes this work. We encourage you to protect the 9–10 PM window as much as possible.

Is Session 1 really free? What about the rest?

Yes — Session 1 on June 16 is completely free. No payment, no credit card, nothing. After Session 1, if you choose to continue, Sessions 2–10 are available for ₹399 total. Payment details will be shared in the WhatsApp group after the first session.
